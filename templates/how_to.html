[ QUICK ANSWERS ]

Common questions

Is my PDF data secure?
Yes. All uploads use SSL/TLS encryption. Files are processed in isolated environments and automatically deleted from our servers after 60 minutes. We never access, share, or store your document contents beyond the conversion window.
What happens to my file after conversion?
Both your uploaded PDF and the converted DOCX are automatically purged from our servers 60 minutes after upload. We recommend downloading your converted file immediately. There is no way for us (or anyone else) to retrieve your files after they're deleted.
Does it work on scanned PDFs?
Yes. Our PaddleOCR engine is designed specifically for scanned documents, images, and PDFs with embedded text. It achieves 95%+ accuracy on clear scans. Best results come from PDFs with good contrast, straight pages, and readable text.
What's the file size limit?
20MB per file. This covers the vast majority of PDF documents — including multipage reports, presentations, and ebooks. If your file is larger, try splitting it into smaller sections before uploading.
Can I convert multiple PDFs at once?
You can queue multiple conversions sequentially — upload one, start the conversion, then upload the next while the first processes. Each conversion runs independently. Full batch upload is coming in a future update.
